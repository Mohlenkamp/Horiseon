Rolling Stones Fan Page

While picking only one favorite band or musician is like eating one potato chip, I'd have to say that the Rolling Stones have got to be my first chip.

Why them?

  • They can play rock, blues, pop, country, and even gospel.
  • They have created iconic songs that span both nations and generations.
  • They're still all together(for the most part) and making music.
  • They have been doing it for 7 decades.

Bio

Mick Jagger - Lead Singer
